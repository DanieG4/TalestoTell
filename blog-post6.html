Vegitarian Diaries: Tokyo Edition

When planning my trip to Tokyo, the eorry of what I would eat never really crossed my mind. I was just thinking about the endless amounts of ramen, sushi and sweets I would be stuffing into my system while I walked the city streets. With the help of google translate, I thought it would be easy to pick out food and resturants that had vegitarian options. While in a way it was, I also had many moments of struggle when it came to eating out wuick before the next adventure. Tokyo has thousands of resturants, small eateries and vendors to choose from. Im here to tell my fellow vegitarians that not all hope is lost! You indeed do not have to struggle to find a true vegitarian ramen in one of thier many districts, because I have found them all.
