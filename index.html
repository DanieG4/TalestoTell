Hey everyone!

Welcome to my travel corner! I am SO glad you are here! As a girl in my late 20s with a serious case of wanderlust, this blog is my little corner of the internet where I share my tales from the road- filled with thrilling adventures, candid experiences, and the lessons learned along the way. The people I have met, the places I have seen, the troubles I have overcome- I believe they all have a story that is worth sharing.

Tales to tell is all about the journey, the small moments that have turned into the biggest memories. Here you will not only find stories from my personal experiences, but from those who I have met along the way. I hope you find this blog to be a comforting and safe space to experience the world with me. After all, we all have a tale to tell!
