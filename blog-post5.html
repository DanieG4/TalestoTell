Chasing the peak: Vail

Vail is a resort known around the world and its lore truly knows no bounds. When I lived back east, it was actually one of the only mountains I had heard of in Colorado when I was young. Its known the be prestine, fancy, expensive a rival to the famous Aspen mountain. The perfectly manicured cobblestone streets and high priced storefronts sure do make this live up to the name. However, there really is more to vail then meets the eye. The lengendary back bowls really hold thier name- legendary. On a fesh powder day, I would argue there really is no better place to be.
