About me

Hello fellow wanderlust friends!

I am New England native who moved to the rockies in search of adventure- and boy did I find it! I am an outdoor enthusiest who thrives on each adventure and experience I am lucky enough to find. Growing up, I was limited in where I could go but daydreamed of all the places I have yet to see. (yes I was that person dropping my google guy all over the world and standing in the travel section of barnes and noble for just a little too long) My inspiration for travel comes from a very vulnerable place. I learned from a very young age how short life is with the loss of many important people in my life. After feeling stuck for a lot of my life, I am finally able to fuel that wanderlust energy.

When Im not enjoying the stunning landscapes outside of my door, you can find me plotting my next adventure. Whether I'm getting lost in a new city or trying to communicate with locals in a language I barely know, I embrace every moment (and every mishap!) that travel brings.

I cant wait to share my experiences with you all. All of the good and all of the real! I aim to show you the true sides of traveling through first hand stories and experiences. Thanks for joining!
